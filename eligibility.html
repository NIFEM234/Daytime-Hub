Referral & Eligibility Criteria

Last updated: 30 January 2026

DayTime Hub provides a safe, welcoming space and practical support for adults who are experiencing from professionals.

Who can access DayTime Hub services?

  • Adults (18+) experiencing homelessness or housing insecurity.
  • People sleeping rough, in temporary accommodation, or at imminent risk of homelessness.
  • People in need of basic support such as meals, showers, laundry, clothing, or advice.

How to refer

  • Self‑referral: Drop in during opening hours or contact us using the details below.
  • Professional referral: Agencies and support workers can refer via email or phone.

What to bring

  • Any form of ID if available (not required).
  • Basic contact details so we can follow up if needed.
  • Information about support needs or risk factors you wish to share.

Safeguarding and safety

We operate a safe space policy. Aggressive or threatening behaviour is not tolerated. Where needed, we may involve partner agencies to ensure safety and appropriate support.

Not sure if someone is eligible?

Contact us and we will advise on eligibility, alternatives, and the best next steps.
