About DayTime Hub

A warm space where everyone is treated with dignity and respect

Our Story

The Daytime Hub has been part of the Gravesend Methodist Church since 2010 when the need to help our local homeless community was discovered. What began as a warm space for a cup of tea grew over a number of years into something much more.

Today, we provide a warm shower, clean clothing, a hot breakfast and a safe space to feel like a member of the community. We offer advice and support from local agencies, and our dedicated team is trained to listen and spot signs of exploitation.

We are a "drop-in" centre situated in the centre of Gravesend â€“ no two days are ever the same, but our commitment to helping those in need remains constant.

Our Mission

The Daytime Hub mission is to help, encourage and motivate those who are vulnerable due to homelessness, addiction, mental health, domestic abuse and exploitation.

We humanise homelessness, befriend the lost and feed the hungry.

What we offer

We offer a comprehensive range of support services to help our guests with their immediate needs while working towards longer-term solutions.

  • Full English Breakfast
  • Hot showers and laundry facilities
  • Clean clothing and essentials
  • Food bank access
  • Housing support and advice
  • Addiction and dependency support
  • Hepatitis C testing and treatment
  • Mental health support
  • Counselling services (when grants allow)
  • First aid trained staff
  • Naloxone overdose reversal training
