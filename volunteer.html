Volunteer FAQs

A few quick answers to help you get started.

How much time do I need to commit?

We are open Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. You can volunteer weekly or as your schedule allows.

What training do you provide?

We provide an induction and role-specific guidance so you feel confident and supported.

Do I need a DBS check?

Some roles may require a DBS check. We will discuss this with you and guide you through the process if needed.
