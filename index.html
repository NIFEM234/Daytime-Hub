The Daytime Hub mission is to help, encourage and motivate those who are vulnerable due to homelessness,
addiction, mental health issues, domestic abuse and exploitation. We provide a warm breakfast, showers,laundry,
clothing and so much more.

Award-Winning Support Since 2010

What began as a warm space for a cup of tea has grown into a vital community hub providing support to hundreds of vulnerable people in Gravesend.

Due to being the only homeless charity in Kent that stayed open during Covid, we were awarded the Pandemic Hero Award (2020) and the Gravesham Civic and Community Award (2022). We were also invited to the Royal Garden Party for our outstanding work.
